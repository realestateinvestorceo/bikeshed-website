What We Do

Services

Tune-Ups

From basic adjustments to full overhauls. We'll dial in your shifting, brakes, and bearings so your ride feels brand new.

Brake Service

Brake bleeds, pad replacements, cable adjustments, and full brakeset installs. Stop on a dime, every time.

Tires & Wheels

Tire installs, tube replacements, insert setups, and wheel truing. We stock Maxxis and more.

Suspension

Parts & Gear

Components, accessories, apparel, and protection kits from the brands you trust. In stock and ready to go.

Ride Wrap

Frame protection kits professionally installed. Keep your bike looking fresh no matter how gnarly the trail gets.
